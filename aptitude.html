  • 42 km/h

  • ₹8000
  • ₹8500
  • ₹9000
  • ₹8800

OPTION: C — SI = P × R × T / 100 = P × 10 × 2 / 100 = 0.2P; P + 0.2P = 9800 → P = 9000.

  • 4 days
  • 5 days
  • 6 days
  • 3 days

OPTION: A — 4 men = 6 women → 1 man = 1.5 women; 8 men + 12 women = 24 women; time = 12 × (6/24) = 3 days.

  • ₹1,800
